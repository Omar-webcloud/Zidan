About Zidan

Designing with a sharp eye for story, type, and visual rhythm.

Zidan Sharma is a Graphic Designer and Visualizer based in Chittagong, Bangladesh. Working with ArtShala Global, he focuses on crafting strong visual systems, expressive brand communication, and design assets that connect ideas with audiences.

His work blends layout, typography, color, composition, and storytelling to create visuals that feel polished, purposeful, and memorable.
